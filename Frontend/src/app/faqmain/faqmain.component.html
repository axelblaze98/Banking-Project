2. What is special about Internet banking?

Internet banking is the most convenient way to bank- anytime, any place, at your convenience.

3. I do not have an account with Bank of LTI?

You are welcome to open it now. It is very easy to open an account with Bank of LTI. You can apply online for opening of a savings bank account. 4. I want to register for OnlineLTI now. What do I do?

Fill up the Registration Form on the OnlineLTI site, complete the form and submit it. Your registration formalities are complete after your details are verified and authenticated by the Admin.

5. Can I change my password?

Passwords can be changed any time and any number of times. In fact we recommend that your password should be changed periodically to secure access to your account information.

6. What happens if I forget my login password?

Click on the 'Forgot password' link in the site and provide the requested information. You can reset your password after authentication.
